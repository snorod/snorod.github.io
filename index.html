FAQ

  • ARE YOU TRULY PASSIONATE ABOUT IGN?

    Absolutely. I didn't talk too much about that in my video, because I figured many applicants go on and on about it. I read almost every IGN article and I watch Beyond(!), Up At Noon, and Anime Club. I'm a fan of Max, Brian, everyone else on Beyond, Destin Legarie, and above all Adam Sligar and Francisco Velazquez since they're part of Code-Foo and might be reading this.

  • ARE YOU REALLY AS BLAND IN PERSON AS YOU ARE IN YOUR VIDEO?

    Boy, I sure hope not. Talking to a camera is inherently awkward; I'm not sure how vloggers do it.

  • WHY SHOULD IGN HIRE YOU IF YOUR WEBSITE IS UNIMPRESSIVE?

    My website changes all the time because I mainly use it as a playground for trying things out, so it's not always an accurate indication of my skill level.

  • WHY DIDN'T YOU DO MORE OF THE PROGRAMMING CHALLENGES?

    Believe me, I really wanted to. I worked on my Code-Foo application whenever I had a spare moment (even during spring break!); I just haven't had enough time to do much outside of what's required.

  • WHY IS YOUR GITHUB SO EMPTY?

    A lot of the work I do is on a private Georgia Tech Github. I also don't push all of my code to GitHub anyway.

  • WHAT'S YOUR FAVORITE THING ABOUT IGN?

    The reason that I've been a daily visitor for so many years, I think, is that IGN is great at knowing what content its audience wants to see. IGN's evolution past "video game news" is one of the main reasons it's remained relevant. Now, I can get all the information I care about just from IGN.

  • ARE YOU REALLY GOOD AT HANDSTANDS?

    It's funny that you ask! I AM really good at handstands. I can also dislocate a lot of things in my body. Isn't that something you'd like to see in person?
